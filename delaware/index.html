Official Certification: Certified Peer Recovery Specialist (CPRS)

Certification Overview

The CPRS credential is for individuals with personal, lived experience in their own substance use disorder and/or mental health disorder recovery who wish to help others on their recovery journey.

Certifying Bodies

Delaware Certification Board
Website: decertboard.org

Delaware Division of Substance Abuse and Mental Health (DSAMH)
Website: DSAMH Peer Services

Training Requirements: 46 Hours

Domain Hour Requirements:

  • Advocacy: 10 hours
  • Mentoring/Education: 10 hours
  • Recovery/Wellness Support: 10 hours
  • Ethical Responsibility: 16 hours

Work Experience

  • Total Hours: 500 hours work or volunteer experience as a peer recovery specialist
  • Must be properly documented and verified

Written Requirements

Statement of Lived Experience: 1,000-word essay describing your experience with recovery, history of sustained recovery, and how experience informs peer support work

2025 Training Schedule

Delaware Certified Peer Support Specialist Training

Duration: Nine-day training

Frequency: Offered twice a year

Cost: NO COST to participants (state-funded)

Format: May be in-person, virtual, or hybrid

Schedule: Visit mha-in-de.corsizio.com

Primary Training Provider

Mental Health Association in Delaware (MHA)

Website: mentalhealthde.com/peer-services
Training Portal: mha-in-de.corsizio.com

Programs Offered:
  • CPRS Training: Nine-day advanced training (twice yearly, NO COST)
  • Peer 101: Two-day introductory training (monthly)
  • Supervising Peer Recovery Specialists: One-day training (quarterly)
  • Continuing Education: Monthly CEU opportunities
Eligibility: At least 18 years of age, High School Diploma or GED, Direct personal experience living with mental illness and/or addiction and recovery

Application Process

  1. Meet eligibility requirements (18+, HS Diploma/GED, lived experience)
  2. Apply for CPRS Training through MHA
  3. Complete nine-day training (46 hours covering all domains)
  4. Accumulate 500 work/volunteer hours
  5. Write 1,000-word statement of lived experience
  6. Submit certification application to Delaware Certification Board
  7. Receive CPRS certification

Key Features

  • Training provided at NO COST
  • Nine-day comprehensive training format
  • Monthly CEU opportunities
  • Strong emphasis on ethical responsibility (16 of 46 hours)
  • 1,000-word reflective essay requirement

Employment Opportunities

Peer services may be Medicaid billable in Delaware. Employment opportunities in substance use disorder treatment facilities, community mental health centers, integrated behavioral health programs, hospital-based programs, community-based organizations, correctional and reentry programs, and recovery community centers.
