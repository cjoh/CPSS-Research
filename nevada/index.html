Certification Overview

Nevada's Peer Recovery and Support Specialist (PRSS) certification is managed by the Nevada Certification Board. Certified PRSS professionals must have lived experience in recovery and complete comprehensive training and examination requirements.

Nevada Certification Board (NCB)

Website: nevadacertboard.org/certs/prss/requirements

Requirements

Education Requirements

  • High school diploma or jurisdictionally certified high school equivalency (HiSet or GED)

Personal Recovery Requirements

  • In recovery for at least two years prior to certification

Residency Requirements

  • Must live or work in Nevada at least 51% of the time to be certified and maintain certification

Training Requirements

  • Successful completion of one of three NCB approved, Nevada State sanctioned PRSS 101 foundational training courses
  • 46 hours of NCB approved training specific to Peer Recovery and Support domains
  • 16 hours in the domain of Ethical Responsibility

Work Experience Requirements

  • 500 hours of volunteer or paid work experience specific to the domains
  • 25 hours of supervised experience

Examination

  • Pass the IC&RC Peer Recovery Examination

Application Process

  1. Complete Training: Complete 46-hour NCB approved PRSS training
  2. Gain Work Experience: Complete 500 hours volunteer/paid experience (including 25 supervised hours)
  3. Submit Application: Apply through Nevada Certification Board Certemy system
  4. Pass IC&RC Exam: Successfully complete IC&RC Peer Recovery Examination

Fees and Costs

Fee Type Cost
Initial Certification (includes IC&RC exam) $165
Recertification (every two years) $100

Renewal Requirements

  • Renewal Period: Every two years
  • Continuing Education: 20 hours in IC&RC Peer Recovery Domains (6 hours must be in ethics)

Training Providers

Foundation for Recovery

  • Website: forrecovery.org/education-and-training

CASAT (Center for the Application of Substance Abuse Technologies)

  • Website: casat.org - Peer Support Specialist Certificate

Processing Time

Allow up to 10 business days for approvals of any application steps/submissions in your online application.
