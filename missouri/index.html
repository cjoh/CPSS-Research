Certification Overview

Missouri's Certified Peer Specialist (CPS) program provides certification for individuals in personal recovery who want to support others. The Missouri Department of Mental Health, Division of Behavioral Health recognizes the Missouri Credentialing Board as the sole source for certified peer credentials.

Missouri Credentialing Board

Website: missouricb.com

Training Site: mopeerspecialist.com

Requirements

Education Requirements

  • Minimum of high school level education

Personal Recovery Requirements

  • Be in personal recovery from a substance use disorder, mental health disorder, or both
  • No specific time frame of recovery required

Training Requirements

  • Complete 5-day Basic Training Program (two schedule options available)
  • All sessions online
  • Access to computer, laptop or tablet required (cell phone only is not acceptable)
  • Pass State of Missouri approved certification examination

Application Process

  1. Complete Application: Submit training application at mopeerspecialist.com or through Missouri Credentialing Board
  2. Pay Fee: $75 fee covers training, testing, and certification
  3. Complete Training: Attend and complete 5-day training program
  4. Pass Examination: Complete State of Missouri approved exam
  5. Submit Certification Application: Submit with required documentation

Fees and Costs

  • Training, Testing, and Certification Fee: $75 (collected at registration)

Renewal Requirements

  • Renewal Period: Every two years
  • Continuing Education: 20 hours total, including 6 hours of "live" ethics training
