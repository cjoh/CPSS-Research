# Montana Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** LEGALLY DEFINED IN STATUTE - Licensure Required

**Title:** Certified Behavioral Health Peer Support Specialist (CBHPSS)

**Definition:** MCA ยง 37-39-312 and administrative rules define "behavioral health peer support" as:

> "The use of personal experience with a behavioral health disorder to provide support, mentoring, guidance, and advocacy to individuals with behavioral health disorders while under the supervision of a mental health professional."

**Citation:** Montana Code Annotated ยง 37-39-312; Administrative Rules 24.219.912

## Coaching vs. Therapy
Peer support is distinct from clinical treatment. Provides support, mentoring, guidance based on lived experience. Must work under supervision.

## Independent Practice Requirements
**Status:** LICENSURE REQUIRED - Cannot Practice Without License

**Critical:** 
> "A person may not practice peer support unless licensed under Title 37, chapter 1, and this chapter."

**SUPERVISION REQUIRED:**
Must work "under the supervision of a mental health professional in an amount, duration, and scope appropriate to the setting."

**Qualified Supervisors:**
- LCSW, LCPC, LMFT, LAC
- Physician, Psychologist
- APRN with psychiatric mental health specialty
- Must have 3 years licensed experience OR board-approved clinical supervision training

**Licensure Requirements:**
- Age 18+
- High school diploma/GED/college transcript
- Diagnosis from mental health professional of behavioral health disorder
- Received treatment for diagnosed disorder
- In recovery (2 years, not hospitalized >72 hours, not incarcerated)
- 40 hours board-approved training (including ethics)
- Pass examination

**Renewal:**
- Annual renewal ($110 fee)
- 20 hours CE annually

**Regulatory Body:** Montana Board of Behavioral Health

**Citation:** MCA ยง 37-39-312; Mont. Admin. R. 24.219.912

## Summary
- **Legally defined?** YES - in statute, licensure required
- **Coaching vs. therapy?** Distinct; peer support under supervision
- **Independent practice?** NO - Licensure and supervision required
- **Interventionists different?** No information found

## Practical Implications
Montana REQUIRES LICENSURE - one of few states with this requirement.

**Must:**
- Obtain state license
- Work under licensed mental health professional supervision
- Complete 40-hour board-approved training
- Maintain 20 CE hours annually
- Cannot practice without license

**Cannot:**
- Practice without license (illegal)
- Practice without supervision
- Operate independently

**Contact:** Montana Board of Behavioral Health: boards.bsd.dli.mt.gov/behavioral-health
