# Maine Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** Certified Peer Recovery Coach (CPRC)

**Administered by:** Maine Recovery Coach Certification Board

**Mission:** "Standardizes and promotes best practice qualifications... defining and maintaining highest standards of professional practice and ethics whether in volunteer or employed capacity."

## Coaching vs. Therapy
Recovery coaches guide and support anyone entering or sustaining recovery from addiction. Distinct from clinical treatment.

## Independent Practice Requirements
**Status:** Certification available; both volunteer and employed opportunities

**Training:** CCAR Recovery Coach Academy - 5-day intensive training, provides skills to guide and support recovery.

**Free Training:** Thanks to Office of Behavioral Health funding, CCAR training courses FREE to Maine residents.

**Requirements:**
- Complete CCAR or equivalent training
- Demonstrate knowledge/skills in four CPRC performance domains (IC&RC Peer Recovery Candidate Guide)
- Apply for certification

**Opportunities:**
- Volunteer opportunities: Community Recovery Coaching program
- Paid positions: Maine Recovery Core internships

**Citation:** Maine Recovery Coach Certification Board standards

## Summary
- **Legally defined?** Through state certification board
- **Coaching vs. therapy?** Peer support for addiction recovery
- **Independent practice?** Both volunteer and paid opportunities
- **Interventionists different?** No information found

## Key Points
- State certification board established
- Free training for Maine residents
- Both volunteer and paid pathways
- IC&RC competency domains
