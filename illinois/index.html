Official Certification: Certified Recovery Support Specialist (CRSS)

Certification Overview

Illinois offers two main peer recovery certifications: CRSS (state-specific) and CPRS (national IC&RC). CRSS is recognized as a Mental Health Professional (MHP) under Illinois Rule 132 and Rule 140, and can bill Medicaid.

Certifying Bodies

Illinois Certification Board (ICB)
Illinois Department of Human Services (IDHS)
MISA Institute
Website: IDHS Peer Recovery Support Training

Certification Types

Certified Recovery Support Specialist (CRSS)

Recognition: Mental Health Professional (MHP) per Rule 132 & 140

Medicaid Billing: Yes - able to bill Medicaid for services

Scope: Illinois-specific

Certified Peer Recovery Specialist (CPRS)

Recognition: National IC&RC certification

Illinois Status: Not recognized as MHP in Illinois

Scope: Nationally recognized

Training Requirements

Total Program: 16 credits

Training Hours: 100 hours of training and education

Internship: 300 hours in advocacy, mentoring, recovery support, and professional responsibility

Written Exam: Required for CRSS certification

Eligibility Requirements

  • Verification of recovery from mental health or substance use disorder
  • High school diploma or GED
  • Personal knowledge or advocacy in recovery

2025 CRSS Success Program

The IDHS Certified Recovery Support Specialist (CRSS) Success Program is grant-funded through IDHS Division of Behavioral Health and Recovery (DBHR).

Program Includes:

  • Classroom work and internship
  • Tuition assistance
  • Books and materials
  • Transportation support
  • Computer equipment
  • Childcare assistance
  • Internship stipend
  • Student success coach

Training Providers

Post-Secondary Educational Institutions offering CRSS Programs:

  • Elgin Community College: Recovery Support Specialist
  • College of DuPage: RSS Certificate
  • Illinois Institute of Technology: RSS Certificate
  • Governors State University: CRSS Training
  • Northern Illinois University: RSS Program
  • College of Lake County: RSS Certificate Program

Certification Exams

  • CPRS Exam: 75 multiple-choice questions
  • CRSS Exam: 100 multiple-choice questions
  • Exam Fee: $125
  • Time Allowed: 2 hours (computer-based exam)
