# Colorado Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** Legally defined in statute

**Definition:** C.R.S. ยง 27-60-108 defines "peer support professional" to include recovery coaches.

**Citation:** Colorado Revised Statutes ยง 27-60-108 (enacted 2021, HB 21-1021)

## Coaching vs. Therapy
Peer support professionals help people achieve recovery goals through shared understanding, respect, and empowerment. Distinct from clinical treatment.

## Independent Practice
**Status:** Certification NOT required to work (as of 2024)

**IMPORTANT CHANGE:** Effective January 1, 2026, all Peer Support Professionals delivering Medicaid behavioral health services must be credentialed by nationally recognized organization (by July 1, 2026).

**Current Requirements:**
- Combined Core Competencies training (state requirement)
- COPA (Colorado Providers Association) certification available but not mandatory
- IC&RC credential provides portability

**Citation:** C.R.S. ยง 27-60-108; HCPF regulations
