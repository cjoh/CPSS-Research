Certification Overview

New Hampshire offers a Peer Support Specialist certificate program through the community college network in collaboration with DHHS and Dartmouth Health. The program provides formal state certification upon completion.

Free Training Through March 2025

The course is subsidized by NH DHHS and free to attendees through March 2025.

New Hampshire DHHS - Office of Consumer & Family Affairs

Website: dhhs.nh.gov

Program Structure

  • 40-46 hour certificate program comprising nine (9) course modules
  • Delivered over ten (10) weeks
  • Virtual delivery via asynchronous and live-online teaching
  • Weekly online learning modules plus weekly interactive virtual meetings

Requirements

  • Complete all 9 modules with built-in assessments
  • Attend all nine (9) weekly virtual Community of Practice sessions

Training Providers

Community College System of New Hampshire

  • Lakes Region Community College
  • River Valley Community College
  • White Mountains Community College
