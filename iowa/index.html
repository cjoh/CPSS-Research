Official Certification: Peer Recovery Specialist (PRS)

Certification Overview

Iowa offers Peer Recovery Specialist (PRS) certification administered by the Iowa Board of Certification (IBC) for individuals with lived experience in substance use disorder recovery or as family members of those in recovery.

Certifying Body

Iowa Board of Certification (IBC)
Phone: 515-965-5509
Email: info@iowabc.org
Website: iowabc.org
PRS Handbook & Application: iowabc.org/prs

Eligibility Requirements

  • Have a substance use disorder and be living well in recovery, OR
  • Be a family member of a person in recovery from a substance use disorder

Training Requirements

Certificate of completion must be submitted for:

  • Iowa Peer Support Specialist Training (University of Iowa)
  • Georgia Model Training (IPSTA)
  • Life Connections Training
  • Equivalent Model approved by IBC

Application Process

  1. Meet eligibility requirements (lived experience or family member)
  2. Complete approved training program (Iowa PSS, Georgia Model, Life Connections, or IBC-approved equivalent)
  3. Download PRS Application and Handbook from iowabc.org/prs
  4. Submit application with training certificate
  5. Contact IBC office with questions: 515-965-5509 or info@iowabc.org
  6. Receive PRS certification
