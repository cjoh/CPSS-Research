Recovery Coaching Legal Guide

Legal Requirements & Regulations for Independent Recovery Coaching Practice Across All 51 U.S. States

About This Guide

This comprehensive guide provides critical legal information about recovery coaching regulations in all 50 states plus the District of Columbia. Recovery coaching is NOT the same as peer support specialist certification. This research focuses specifically on the legal requirements, boundaries, and restrictions for independent recovery coaching practice.

Research Completed: November 2025 | All 51 jurisdictions researched

⚠️ Critical Disclaimer

This information is for educational purposes only and does not constitute legal advice. Laws and regulations change frequently. Before starting a recovery coaching practice, you must:

  • Consult with an attorney licensed in your state
  • Verify current regulations with your state's licensing board
  • Understand the specific boundaries between coaching and licensed therapy
  • Obtain appropriate liability insurance
  • Never make claims or engage in activities requiring professional licensure
