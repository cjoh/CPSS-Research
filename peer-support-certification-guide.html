Peer Support Specialist Certification Guide

Comprehensive Resource for Addiction Recovery Peer Support Certification Across All 51 U.S. States & Territories

About This Guide

This comprehensive guide provides detailed information about becoming a certified peer support specialist for addiction recovery in all 50 states plus the District of Columbia. Each state has its own certification process, requirements, and training providers. Use the search and filter tools below to find information specific to your state.
