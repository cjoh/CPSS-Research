# Mississippi Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** Certified Peer Support Specialist Professional (CPSSP)

**Definition:** One who has successfully completed CPSSP Training provided through MS Department of Mental Health (DMH).

**Training Designations:**
- Adult and Recovery
- Parent/Caregiver
- Young Adult
- Forensic Peer Recovery
- Recovery only (if previously trained pre-May 2021)

**Administered by:** Mississippi Department of Mental Health, Division of Professional Licensure and Certification (PLACE)

## Coaching vs. Therapy
Provide peer support services under general supervision of mental health professional. Non-clinical support, not therapy.

**Scope includes:**
- Advocacy
- Education
- Development of natural supports
- Support of work/meaningful activity
- Crisis support
- Wellness activities
- Service coordination and linkage

## Independent Practice Requirements
**Status:** CANNOT PRACTICE INDEPENDENTLY

**EMPLOYMENT REQUIREMENT:**
> "Employment in Mississippi's Public Mental Health system is mandatory to hold the certification."

> "Employment in Mississippi's 'state mental health system,' as defined above, is a mandatory requirement to apply for and hold a DMH professional credential."

**Requirements:**
- Age 16+ (with HS/GED or enrolled in school/GED program)
- Attest to 6 of last 12 months in self-directed recovery
- 100 hours paid/volunteer work experience (last 3 years)
- 34-hour training course
- Pass exam with 75% minimum
- Employed within DMH-funded/certified/licensed program
- Under general supervision of mental health professional

**Renewal:** 5 hours CE per year

**Citation:** Mississippi Code Title 24, Part 3; DMH CPSSP Program guidelines

## Summary
- **Legally defined?** YES - through state certification
- **Coaching vs. therapy?** Non-clinical peer support under supervision
- **Independent practice?** NO - Must be employed in state mental health system
- **Interventionists different?** No information found

## Practical Implications
Mississippi has STRICTEST requirement - MUST be employed in state mental health system.

**Cannot:**
- Practice independently
- Work outside state mental health system
- Maintain certification without DMH employment
- Operate private practice

**Must:**
- Be employed by DMH-funded/certified/licensed program
- Work under mental health professional supervision

**Contact:** Mississippi DMH: dmh.ms.gov
