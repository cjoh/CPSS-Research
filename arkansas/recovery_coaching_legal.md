# Arkansas Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** Defined through Arkansas Peer Recovery Program (APRP)

**"The Arkansas Model"** - Three-tiered credentialing process: core, advanced, and supervisor levels

**Administered by:** DHS Office of Substance Abuse and Mental Health (OSAMH) with DHS-approved accrediting entity

## Coaching vs. Therapy
Recovery coaches provide peer support, not clinical treatment.

## Independent Practice
**Status:** Certification required for state-funded positions; independent practice not explicitly addressed

**Requirements:**
- High school diploma/equivalent
- 24 months abstinence-based recovery
- 16 education hours + 500 in-service hours
- Pass 75-question certification test
- 10 hours CE for renewal

**Citation:** Arkansas DHS regulations

## Summary
- **Legally defined?** YES - through state certification program
- **Independent practice?** Not explicitly addressed
- **Interventionists different?** No information found
