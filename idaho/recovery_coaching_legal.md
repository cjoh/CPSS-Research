# Idaho Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** "Recovery coach" defined by Idaho Department of Health and Welfare

**Definition:** "A person who removes barriers for individuals seeking or going through recovery, serving as personal guide and mentor who helps remove barriers and obstacles, linking the recovering person to the recovery community."

**Administered by:** Idaho Board of Alcohol Drug Counselor Certification (IBADCC)

## Coaching vs. Therapy
Recovery coaching is "integral and valuable part of recovery process" - distinct from clinical treatment.

## Independent Practice Requirements
**Status:** Certification required; independent practice not explicitly addressed

**Requirements:**
- Age 18+
- High school diploma or GED
- Pass background check
- Complete approved training

**Certification Body:** IBADCC - member board of IC&RC

**Training:** Idaho Recovery Coach Training Academy provides training, certification support, networking.

**Citation:** Idaho Department of Health and Welfare guidelines; IBADCC standards

## Summary
- **Legally defined?** YES - by state health department
- **Coaching vs. therapy?** Peer support, not clinical treatment
