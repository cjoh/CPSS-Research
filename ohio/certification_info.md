# Ohio Peer Support Specialist Certification Information

## Official Certification Name
**Certified Peer Recovery Supporter (CPRS)**

## Certifying Body
**Ohio Department of Mental Health and Addiction Services (OhioMHAS)**
- Website: https://mha.ohio.gov/community-partners/peer-supporters
- Application Portal: https://elicense.ohio.gov
- Email (Individual Certification): peerservices@mha.ohio.gov
- Email (Provider Certification): peerprovider@mha.ohio.gov

## Certification Overview
The Certified Peer Recovery Supporter (CPRS) credential is for individuals in recovery from mental health and/or substance use disorders who wish to use their lived experience to support others on their recovery journey. CPRS professionals provide support services in various behavioral health settings across Ohio.

**Important 2024 Update:** OhioMHAS announced that incarcerated individuals, as well as those on probation and parole, can now be certified as peer supporters.

## Detailed Requirements

### Eligibility Requirements
- High school diploma or GED (**no waiver or exception** to this requirement)
- Direct lived experience of a mental health and/or substance use disorder
- Currently in recovery from mental health and/or substance use disorder
- No disqualifying criminal offenses

### Training Requirements

#### Step 1: Complete E-Based Academy Courses (16 hours)
Complete **eleven (11) e-Based Academy courses** totaling 16 hours through www.ebasedacademy.org

Prerequisites must be completed before attending the 40-hour live training

#### Step 2: Complete OhioMHAS 40-Hour PRS Training
- **40 hours** of live, interactive training
- Can be completed in-person or online (depending on provider)
- Must be OhioMHAS approved training

### Alternative Training Pathways
Candidates may also apply for certification by:
- Completing an **OhioMHAS-approved training** from recognized organizations
- Having **3 years of lived experience** providing peer support

### Approved Training Organizations Include:
- Appalachian Consulting Arizona Peer Specialist Training
- Clark State Community College Peer Recovery Fundamentals Course
- Connecticut Community for Addiction Recovery (CCAR)
- Depression Bipolar Support Alliance (DBSA) 40-hour training
- National Association of Peer Specialists (N.A.P.S.)

### Background Check Requirements
- **BCI (Bureau of Criminal Investigation)** background check
- **FBI** background check
- Background checks may take **30-45 days** to arrive at OhioMHAS
- Attestation that applicant has not been convicted of any new felony offenses required at renewal

### Code of Ethics
- Certified peer supporters must attest to having read and understood the Code of Ethics
- Required at initial certification and every certification renewal

## Application Process

### Step 1: Complete Prerequisites
Complete all eleven e-Based Academy courses (16 hours) at www.ebasedacademy.org

### Step 2: Complete Training
Complete OhioMHAS 40-Hour PRS Training through an approved provider

### Step 3: Background Checks
Complete BCI and FBI background checks (allow 30-45 days for processing)

### Step 4: Apply for Certification
1. Create account in eLicense web portal: https://elicense.ohio.gov
2. Complete online application
3. Upload training certificates
4. Submit background check results
5. Pay application fee

### Step 5: Pass Examination
- Pass the online **Ohio Peer Recovery Supporter Certification Exam**
- Passing score: **70% or higher**

### Step 6: Receive Certification
Receive certification via email through eLicense portal within **14 business days** after passing exam

## Application Fees
- **Initial Application Fee:** Contact OhioMHAS for current fee information
- **Examination Fee:** Included in application process

## Renewal Requirements

### Renewal Period
Certifications must be renewed **annually**

### Continuing Education Requirements
**30 hours** of continuing education credits per renewal period, including:

#### Required Topic Areas (with minimum hours):
1. **Ethics** (including HIPAA, confidentiality): **3 hours minimum**
2. Additional 8 topic areas with specific hour requirements
3. Remaining CE hours in any topic relevant to human services or Peer Support

### Supervisor Training (if applicable)
For certified peer supporters providing supervision: **3 hours** of supervisor training documentation required

### Renewal Process
1. Complete required continuing education hours
2. Submit renewal application through eLicense portal
3. Attest to reading and understanding Code of Ethics
4. Submit attestation of no new felony convictions OR new background check
5. Pay renewal fee

### Renewal Fees
- **Standard Renewal Fee:** Contact OhioMHAS for current fee information
- **Late Renewal:** Additional fees may apply for late renewals

### Renewal Resources
Visit: https://mha.ohio.gov/community-partners/peer-supporters/certified-peer-recovery-supporter-cprs-adult/apply-for-peer-certification-renewal

## How to Become a Certified Training Provider

### Overview
To become an approved training provider or facilitator for OhioMHAS CPRS programs, organizations must work with OhioMHAS.

### Training Provider Requirements
- Must offer OhioMHAS-approved 40-hour curriculum
- Trainers must be qualified and approved by OhioMHAS
- Must meet state standards for peer recovery support training

### Application Process
**Contact Information:**
- **Email:** peerprovider@mha.ohio.gov (for provider certification questions)
- **Website:** https://mha.ohio.gov/community-partners/peer-supporters

### Becoming a Facilitator
OhioMHAS periodically seeks facilitators for paid PEER trainings. Interested individuals should:
1. Monitor OhioMHAS announcements and communications
2. Contact OhioMHAS directly about facilitator opportunities
3. Meet qualifications established by OhioMHAS

### Training Standards
- Must use OhioMHAS-approved curriculum
- Must complete 40 hours of instruction
- Must prepare participants for Ohio certification exam
- Must align with OhioMHAS competencies and standards

### Continuing Education Provider Approval
Organizations wishing to provide continuing education for CPRS renewal:
- **Website:** https://www.ce-credit.com/approvals/board/387/ohio-department-of-mental-health-and-addiction-services
- Must receive approval from OhioMHAS for CE offerings
- Must align with required topic areas for CPRS renewal
