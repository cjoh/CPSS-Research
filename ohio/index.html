Official Certification: Certified Peer Recovery Supporter (CPRS)

Certification Overview

The Certified Peer Recovery Supporter (CPRS) credential is for individuals in recovery from mental health and/or substance use disorders who wish to use their lived experience to support others on their recovery journey. CPRS professionals provide support services in various behavioral health settings across Ohio.

Important 2024 Update

OhioMHAS announced that incarcerated individuals, as well as those on probation and parole, can now be certified as peer supporters.

Certifying Body

Ohio Department of Mental Health and Addiction Services (OhioMHAS)
Application Portal: elicense.ohio.gov
Email (Individual): peerservices@mha.ohio.gov
Email (Provider): peerprovider@mha.ohio.gov
Website: mha.ohio.gov/community-partners/peer-supporters

Requirements

Eligibility Requirements

  • Education: High school diploma or GED (no waiver or exception)
  • Direct lived experience of a mental health and/or substance use disorder
  • Currently in recovery from mental health and/or substance use disorder
  • No disqualifying criminal offenses

Training Requirements

Step 1: Complete 11 e-Based Academy courses (16 hours) at www.ebasedacademy.org

Step 2: Complete OhioMHAS 40-Hour PRS Training (live, interactive training)

Alternative: OhioMHAS-approved training from recognized organizations or 3 years of lived experience providing peer support

Background Check Requirements

  • BCI (Bureau of Criminal Investigation) background check
  • FBI background check
  • Background checks may take 30-45 days to arrive at OhioMHAS
  • Attestation of no new felony offenses required at renewal

Application Process

1

Complete Prerequisites

Complete all 11 e-Based Academy courses (16 hours) at www.ebasedacademy.org

2

Complete Training

Complete OhioMHAS 40-Hour PRS Training through an approved provider

3

Background Checks

Complete BCI and FBI background checks (allow 30-45 days for processing)

4

Apply & Test

Apply via elicense.ohio.gov and pass the Ohio Peer Recovery Supporter Certification Exam (70% passing score)

Apply via eLicense e-Based Academy

Renewal Requirements

Renewal Period: Annual

Continuing Education: 30 hours per renewal period, including minimum 3 hours in Ethics (including HIPAA, confidentiality)

Supervisor Training: 3 hours required if providing supervision
