Certification Overview

New Mexico's Certified Peer Support Worker (CPSW) program is managed by the Office of Peer Recovery and Engagement (OPRE) within the New Mexico Health Care Authority. Training is provided at no cost to eligible participants.

2025 Training Registration Opens June 30, 2025

Training dates: August 18-22, 2025. Registration opens Monday, June 30th, 2025 at 8am for up to 50 people total.

New Mexico Office of Peer Recovery and Engagement (OPRE)

Website: OPRE - Become a CPSW

Certification Board: nmcbbhp.org

Requirements

  • Must be 18 years of age
  • Valid high school diploma, GED, or equivalent
  • Self-attest to three or more consecutive years of recovery
  • Valid New Mexico ID and New Mexico home address

Training

  • 40 hours over five days
  • Topics: Professionalism, ethics, recovery principles, mental health, substance use, communication, stress management, supportive housing
  • Pass exam with score of 75% or better
