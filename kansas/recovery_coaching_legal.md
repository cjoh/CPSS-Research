# Kansas Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** Kansas Certified Peer Specialist (KCPS)

**Administered by:** Kansas Department for Aging and Disability Services (KDADS)

**Related:** Kansas Code ยง 39-2019 establishes certified community behavioral health clinics that include peer support services.

**Citation:** Kansas Statutes ยง 39-2019; KDADS certification program

## Coaching vs. Therapy
Peer support services within certified community behavioral health clinics. Distinct from clinical treatment.

## Independent Practice Requirements
**Status:** Certification required

**Requirements:**
- Complete provisional certification period (up to 1 year)
- Pass examination
- Satisfy pre-established conditions
- Specified hours dedicated to peer recovery support

**Note:** Kansas uses "Certified Peer Specialist" designation rather than "recovery coach" for similar roles in behavioral health settings.

**Citation:** KDADS Behavioral Health Services Provider Information

## Summary
- **Legally defined?** Through state certification (KCPS)
- **Coaching vs. therapy?** Peer support within behavioral health clinics
- **Independent practice?** Certification required; work within certified clinics
- **Interventionists different?** No information found

## Key Points
- Provisional certification period required
- Work within certified community behavioral health clinics
- State uses "peer specialist" terminology
