# Kansas Peer Support Specialist Certification for Addiction/Substance Use Disorder

## Official Certification Name

## Personal Requirements

### Basic Eligibility
- **Age:** Must be at least 18 years of age or older
- **Recovery Status:** Must self-identify as a person in recovery from a substance use disorder
- **Recovery Time:** Minimum of one (1) year in stable, full recovery from a substance use disorder
- **Lived Experience:** Must have personal lived experience with behavioral health condition

## Education Requirements
No specific educational requirements beyond high school age (18+). However, applicants must be able to complete online and in-person training.

## Training Requirements

### Two-Level Training System

**Level 1: Kansas Certified Peer Specialist in Training**
- Format: BHS approved online (self-paced format)
- Platform: LearnWorlds training platform (kansascpstraining.getlearnworlds.com)
- Exam Requirement: Must achieve score of 80% or greater
- Timeline: If working and billing for peer support services prior to certification, must complete within 4 months of starting position
- Developer: Wichita State University: Community Engagement Institute

**Level 2: Kansas Certified Peer Specialist**
- Format: 2.5 day in-person or virtual training
- Exam Requirement: Must pass exam with 80% proficiency
- Timeline: If working and billing for peer support services prior to certification, must complete within one year of completing Level One
- Frequency: Offered at least 4 times per year in varying locations around the state

## Professional Requirements

### Ethics
- Must agree to and sign the Certified Peer Specialist Code of Ethics

### Credentialing
- Must be credentialed through the KDADS Division of Behavioral Health Services (BHS)
- Credential is required to work as a peer mentor in Kansas

### Background Checks
No specific information provided in certification requirements, but may be required by individual employers.

## Application Process

1. Complete Level 1 online training through approved platform
2. Pass Level 1 exam with 80% or higher
3. Register for Level 2 in-person training
4. Complete Level 2 training (2.5 days)
5. Pass Level 2 exam with 80% proficiency
6. Sign Code of Ethics
7. Apply for credential through KDADS BHS

## Fees
**NO FEE** - There is no fee to obtain the Kansas Certified Peer Mentor credential

## Continuing Education Requirements

### Annual Requirements
- **Minimum:** 6 hours of continuing education units (CEUs) annually
- **Topics may include:**
  - Ethics
  - Boundaries
  - Skill development
  - Cultural competency

## Renewal Requirements
- Complete 6 hours of CEUs annually
- Maintain agreement with Code of Ethics
- Continue to meet personal recovery requirements

## Related Certifications in Kansas

Kansas offers three distinct peer support certifications:

1. **Kansas Certified Peer Mentor (KCPM)** - For substance use disorder (SUD)
2. **Kansas Certified Peer Specialist (KCPS)** - For mental health (MH)
3. **Kansas Certified Parent Peer Support Specialist (KCPPSS)** - For parents with children experiencing behavioral health challenges
