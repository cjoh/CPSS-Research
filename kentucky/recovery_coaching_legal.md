# Kentucky Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** Legally defined in administrative regulations

**Three types:**
1. Adult Peer Support Specialist (908 KAR 2:220)
2. Kentucky Family Peer Support Specialist (908 KAR 2:230)
3. Youth Peer Support Specialist (908 KAR 2:240)

**Definition:** Person with current/past diagnosis of mental health, substance use, or co-occurring disorders providing structured, non-clinical therapeutic activities.

**Administered by:** Kentucky Department of Behavioral Health, Developmental and Intellectual Disabilities (DBHDID)

**Citation:** 908 KAR 2:220, 2:230, 2:240

## Coaching vs. Therapy
**Adult peer support specialist services** means "structured and scheduled non-clinical, therapeutic activities."

Clear distinction from clinical practice.

## Independent Practice Requirements
**Status:** CANNOT PRACTICE INDEPENDENTLY

**SUPERVISION REQUIRED:**
> "A peer support specialist under the supervision of an approved behavioral health practitioner may provide support services."

**Approved Supervisors:**
- Physician, Psychiatrist
- Advanced practice registered nurse
- Physician assistant
- Licensed psychologist
- Licensed psychological practitioner
- Licensed clinical social worker
- Licensed professional clinical counselor
- Licensed marriage and family therapist

**Requirements:**
- 30-hour peer support course (DBHDID-approved)
- Current/past diagnosis qualifying
- Minimum 1 year sobriety (Kentucky requirement)
- Pass oral and written examination
- Work under approved behavioral health practitioner supervision

**Renewal:**
- Annual renewal required
- 6 hours continuing education per year

**Citation:** 908 KAR 2:220

## Summary
- **Legally defined?** YES - in administrative regulations
- **Coaching vs. therapy?** Non-clinical activities, clearly distinguished
- **Independent practice?** NO - Must work under supervision of licensed professional
- **Interventionists different?** No information found

## Practical Implications
Kentucky has CLEAR SUPERVISION REQUIREMENTS - recovery coaches cannot practice independently.

**Must:**
- Work under licensed behavioral health professional
- Obtain state certification
- Complete 30-hour training
- Maintain annual certification (6 CE hours)

**Cannot:**
- Practice independently
- Work without professional supervision
- Operate own practice/LLC without licensed supervisor

**Contact:** Kentucky DBHDID: dbhdid.ky.gov
