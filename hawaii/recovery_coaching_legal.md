# Hawaii Recovery Coaching Legal Requirements
**Research Date:** January 5, 2025

## Legal Definition
**Status:** Hawaii Certified Peer Specialist (HCPS) - Mental Health Focus

**Important Distinction:** Hawaii maintains SEPARATE pathways:
1. **HCPS** - Mental health peer support (cannot have ONLY substance use disorder)
2. **CSAC** - Certified Substance Abuse Counselor (separate certification)

**Definition:** HCPS promote self-determination, personal responsibility, community integration. "Not a licensed clinician and does not possess ability to make diagnoses or provide medication/mental health services."

**Administered by:** Department of Health, Adult Mental Health Division (HCPS); Alcohol and Drug Abuse Division (CSAC)

**Citation:** Hawaii Revised Statutes ยง 321-193(10) for CSAC; AMHD guidelines for HCPS

## Coaching vs. Therapy
Explicitly stated: "Not a licensed clinician." Cannot diagnose or provide clinical services.

## Independent Practice Requirements
**Status:** Must work under supervision

**SUPERVISION REQUIRED:**
> "HCPSs employed by AMHD-funded programs must be under general supervision of mental health professional in accordance with acceptable guidelines and standards."

**HCPS Requirements:**
- Age 18+
- Primary diagnosis of mental health/co-occurring disorder (NOT substance use only)
- Self-identify as receiving mental health services
- Minimum 12 consecutive months self-directed recovery (last 2 years)
- Complete HCPS Training
- Pass oral and written examinations

**Continuing Education:** 16 hours annually (maximum 8 hours online)

**Medicaid Recognition:** Peer support services may be provided to Medicaid consumers by state-certified HCPS.

**Citation:** HCPS Program guidelines; HRS ยง 321-193(10)

## Summary
- **Legally defined?** YES - separate pathways for mental health vs. substance use
- **Coaching vs. therapy?** Clearly distinguished; not clinical
- **Independent practice?** NO - Must work under mental health professional supervision
- **Interventionists different?** No information found

## Key Points
- **Critical:** Single diagnosis of substance use disorder does NOT meet HCPS requirements
- Separate pathway for substance abuse counseling (CSAC)
- Must work under supervision in AMHD-funded programs
- Medicaid-reimbursable services available
